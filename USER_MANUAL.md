## Introduction

TRIPLY is a travel booking platform that allows users to book destinations with a small deposit and unlock their travel calendar for an entire year. The platform also supports activity bookings, merchant partnerships, and affiliate programs.

### Key Features
- **Destination Bookings**: Book destinations with a small deposit (AED 199)
- **Activity Bookings**: Book individual activities at destinations
- **Combined Bookings**: Book a destination and add multiple activities in one transaction
- **Flexible Dates**: Select your travel dates after payment
- **Merchant Program**: Merchants can add and manage activities
- **Admin Dashboard**: Comprehensive management tools for administrators

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Navigate to the TRIPLY homepage
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Register"** in the navigation menu
3. Fill in your details:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm Password
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Check your email for verification link
6. Click the verification link to activate your account

### Logging In

1. Click **"Login"** in the navigation menu
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Forgot Password

1. On the login page, click **"Forgot Password?"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Click the link and set a new password

## User Features

### Browsing Destinations

1. Click **"Destinations"** in the navigation menu
2. Browse available destinations
3. Use the search bar to find specific destinations
4. Click on a destination card to view details

### Viewing Destination Details

1. Click on any destination card
2. View destination information:
   - Description
   - Location
   - Images
   - Price
   - Available activities (if any)
3. Click **"Book Now"** to start booking

### Booking a Destination

1. On the destination detail page, click **"Book Now"**
2. The booking modal will open
3. **Add Activities (Optional)**:
   - Scroll to the "Add Activities" section
   - Browse available activities
   - Click the eye icon to view activity details
   - Click **"Add to Booking"** for each activity you want
   - Selected activities will appear in the summary
4. Review the booking summary:
   - Destination deposit amount
   - Total activity costs (if any)
   - Total amount
5. Click **"Proceed to Payment"**
6. You'll be redirected to the payment page

### Making Payment

1. On the payment page, review your booking details
2. Enter payment information:
   - Card Number
   - Expiry Date
   - CVV
   - Cardholder Name
   - Billing Address
3. Review the total amount (deposit + activities)
4. Click **"Pay Now"**
5. Wait for payment confirmation
6. You'll be redirected to a success page

### Selecting Travel Dates

**Note**: You can select travel dates after making the deposit payment.

1. Go to **"My Bookings"** from the dashboard or navigation
2. Click on the booking you want to add dates to
3. Click **"Select Travel Dates"** button
4. In the calendar modal:
   - Navigate to your preferred month using arrow buttons
   - Click on a start date
   - Click on an end date to complete the range
   - Check **"I'm flexible with these dates (±2 days)"** if applicable
5. Click **"Confirm Dates"**
6. Your dates will be submitted for admin confirmation

### Viewing Your Bookings

1. Click **"My Bookings"** in the navigation or dashboard
2. View all your bookings with:
   - Booking reference
   - Destination name
   - Status
   - Deposit amount
   - Booking date
3. Click on any booking to view details

### Booking Details Page

On the booking details page, you can:

1. **View Booking Information**:
   - Booking reference
   - Destination details
   - Travel dates (if selected)
   - Payment status
   - Booking status

2. **View Linked Activities** (if any):
   - Activity names
   - Selected dates
   - Number of participants
   - Total cost

3. **Actions Available**:
   - **Select Travel Dates**: Choose your travel dates (if deposit paid)
   - **View Destination**: Go to destination page
   - **Cancel Booking**: Cancel your booking (if allowed)

### Booking Activities

You can book activities in two ways:

#### Method 1: Standalone Activity Booking

1. Click **"Activities"** in the navigation menu
2. Browse available activities
3. Click on an activity card to view details
4. Click **"Book Now"**
5. In the booking modal:
   - Select one or multiple dates from the calendar
   - Click **"Continue"**
   - Enter number of participants
   - Enter customer details (name, email, phone, special requests)
   - Review total price
   - Click **"Proceed to Payment"**
6. Complete payment

#### Method 2: Add to Destination Booking

1. When booking a destination, scroll to "Add Activities" section
2. Browse activities for that destination
3. Click the eye icon to view activity details
4. Click **"Add to Booking"**
5. Select dates and number of participants
6. Activity will be added to your destination booking
7. Pay for everything together in one transaction

### User Dashboard

Access your dashboard to:

1. **View Statistics**:
   - Active bookings count
   - Confirmed trips count

2. **Quick Actions**:
   - Create new booking
   - View all bookings

3. **Recent Bookings**:
   - See your latest bookings
   - Click to view details

### Profile Management

1. Click on your profile icon in the navigation
2. Select **"Profile"**
3. View and update:
   - Personal information
   - Email address
   - Password

### Becoming a Merchant

1. Click **"Become a Merchant"** in the navigation menu
2. Fill in the merchant application form:
   - Business name
   - Business description
   - Contact information
3. Submit the application
4. Wait for admin approval
5. Once approved, you'll have access to the merchant dashboard

## Admin Features

### Admin Dashboard

Access the admin dashboard to view:

1. **Overview Statistics**:
   - Total Revenue
   - Total Bookings
   - Total Users
   - Total Affiliates

2. **Booking Statistics**:
   - Total bookings
   - Pending bookings
   - Deposit paid bookings
   - Dates selected bookings
   - Confirmed bookings
   - Rejected bookings
   - Cancelled bookings

3. **Commission Statistics**:
   - Pending commissions
   - Paid commissions
   - Total commissions

4. **Recent Bookings**: Latest booking activity

5. **Quick Actions**:
   - Manage Bookings
   - Destinations
   - Activities
   - Availability
   - Affiliates

### Managing Bookings

1. Navigate to **"Bookings"** from the admin dashboard
2. View all bookings with filters and search
3. For each booking, you can:
   - **View Details**: Click on booking to see full information
   - **Confirm Booking**: Approve the booking after dates are selected
   - **Reject Booking**: Reject if dates are not available
   - **Export**: Export bookings data

### Managing Destinations

1. Navigate to **"Destinations"** from the admin dashboard
2. View all destinations
3. Actions available:
   - **Add New Destination**: Create new destination
   - **Edit Destination**: Update destination details
   - **Delete Destination**: Remove destination
   - **View Details**: See full destination information

### Managing Activities

1. Navigate to **"Activities"** from the admin dashboard
2. Use tabs to switch between:
   - **Pending**: Activities waiting for approval
   - **Approved**: Already approved activities

#### Approving Activities

1. Go to **"Pending"** tab
2. Review activity details:
   - Title and description
   - Location
   - Price and currency
   - Images
   - Merchant information
3. Click **"Approve"** to approve the activity
4. Activity will be visible to customers

#### Rejecting Activities

1. Go to **"Pending"** tab
2. Review activity details
3. Click **"Reject"**
4. Enter rejection reason
5. Click **"Submit"**
6. Merchant will be notified

#### Managing Activity Availability

1. Click **"View Details"** on any activity
2. Click **"Manage Availability"**
3. Select dates on the calendar
4. Set total slots or block/unblock dates
5. Click **"Update Slots"** or **"Block/Unblock Dates"**

### Managing Availability (Destinations)

1. Navigate to **"Availability"** from the admin dashboard
2. Select a destination from the dropdown
3. View the calendar showing:
   - Available dates (white)
   - Fully booked dates (red)
   - Blocked dates
4. **To Update Slots**:
   - Select one or multiple dates
   - Choose slot count (5, 10, 20, 50)
   - Click **"Update Slots"**
5. **To Block/Unblock Dates**:
   - Select dates
   - Click **"Block Dates"** or **"Unblock Dates"**

### Managing Affiliates

1. Navigate to **"Affiliates"** from the admin dashboard
2. View all affiliate users
3. View affiliate statistics:
   - Total commissions
   - Pending commissions
   - Paid commissions
4. **Manage Commissions**:
   - Approve commissions
   - Mark as paid
   - Reject commissions

### Managing Invitations

1. Navigate to **"Invitations"** from the admin dashboard
2. View all sent invitations
3. Actions:
   - **Create New Invitation**: Invite new affiliates
   - **Resend Invitation**: Resend to pending invites
   - **Cancel Invitation**: Cancel pending invites

## Merchant Features

### Merchant Dashboard

Access your merchant dashboard to view:

1. **Earnings Statistics**:
   - Total Earnings
   - Pending Payouts
   - Paid Out
   - Total Bookings

2. **Activity Status**:
   - Pending activities
   - Approved activities
   - Rejected activities

3. **Recent Bookings**: Latest activity bookings

4. **Activity Performance**: Performance metrics for your activities

5. **Quick Actions**:
   - **Manage Availability**: Manage dates and slots for activities
   - **Add Activity**: Create new activity

### Adding an Activity

1. Navigate to **"Add Activity"** from merchant dashboard
2. Fill in the activity form:
   - **Basic Information**:
     - Title (required)
     - Description (required)
     - Location (required)
     - Category
   - **Pricing**:
     - Price (required)
     - Currency (select from dropdown)
   - **Images**:
     - Upload thumbnail image
     - Upload additional images
   - **Additional Details**:
     - Duration
     - Maximum participants
     - What's included
     - What to bring
     - Cancellation policy
3. Click **"Submit for Approval"**
4. Activity will be submitted for admin review
5. You'll be notified when it's approved or rejected

### Managing Activity Availability

1. Navigate to **"Manage Availability"** from merchant dashboard
2. Select an activity from the dropdown (only approved activities)
3. View the calendar showing:
   - Available dates
   - Booked dates
   - Blocked dates
4. **To Update Slots**:
   - Select one or multiple dates
   - Choose slot count (5, 10, 20, 50)
   - Click **"Update Slots"**
5. **To Block/Unblock Dates**:
   - Select dates
   - Click **"Block Dates"** to block dates (e.g., for maintenance)
   - Click **"Unblock Dates"** to make dates available again

**Note**: Blocking dates prevents customers from booking those dates. This is useful for:
- Maintenance days
- Personal time off
- Special events
- Equipment unavailability

### Viewing Activity Bookings

1. On the merchant dashboard, scroll to **"Recent Bookings"**
2. View bookings for your activities:
   - Customer name
   - Activity name
   - Booking date
   - Number of participants
   - Status
   - Amount

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Issues

**Problem**: Payment not processing
- **Solution**: 
  - Check card details are correct
  - Ensure card has sufficient funds
  - Try a different payment method
  - Contact support if issue persists

### Date Selection Issues

**Problem**: Cannot select dates
- **Solution**:
  - Ensure deposit payment is completed
  - Check if dates are available (not fully booked)
  - Try selecting different dates
  - Contact admin if dates show as unavailable

### Booking Status Issues

**Problem**: Booking status not updating
- **Solution**:
  - Refresh the page
  - Wait a few minutes for status to sync
  - Check your email for notifications
  - Contact support if status doesn't update

### Activity Approval Issues

**Problem**: Activity not approved
- **Solution**:
  - Check activity details are complete
  - Ensure images are uploaded
  - Wait for admin review (usually 24-48 hours)
  - Contact admin if rejected without reason

### Availability Management Issues

**Problem**: Cannot update slots or block dates
- **Solution**:
  - Ensure you've selected dates
  - Check you're using an approved activity (merchants)
  - Refresh the page and try again
  - Ensure you have proper permissions

### Login Issues

**Problem**: Cannot log in
- **Solution**:
  - Verify email and password are correct
  - Check if account is verified (check email)
  - Use "Forgot Password" to reset
  - Contact support if account is locked

## Additional Notes

### Booking Statuses

- **Pending**: Booking created, waiting for deposit payment
- **Deposit Paid**: Deposit payment completed
- **Dates Selected**: User has selected travel dates
- **Confirmed**: Admin has confirmed the booking
- **Rejected**: Booking was rejected by admin
- **Cancelled**: Booking was cancelled by user

### Activity Statuses

- **Pending**: Activity submitted, waiting for admin approval
- **Approved**: Activity approved and visible to customers
- **Rejected**: Activity rejected by admin

### Payment Information

- **Deposit Amount**: AED 199 (default, may vary)
- **Payment Methods**: Credit/Debit cards via Stripe
- **Currency**: Supports multiple currencies (AED, USD, EUR, etc.)
- **Refund Policy**: Check individual booking terms

### Calendar Features

- **Date Selection**: Click dates to select
- **Range Selection**: Click start date, then end date
- **Multiple Selection**: Click multiple individual dates
- **Availability**: Red dates are fully booked or blocked
- **White dates**: Available for booking
