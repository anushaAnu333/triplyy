## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Register"** in the navigation bar
2. Fill in your details:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. Check your email for a verification link
5. Click the verification link to activate your account

### Logging In

1. Click **"Login"** in the navigation bar
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Login"**

### Forgot Password

1. Click **"Login"** then **"Forgot Password?"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Click the link and set a new password

## Booking a Destination

### Step 1: Select Destination

1. Browse destinations and click on one you like
2. Review the destination details
3. Click **"Book Now"** button

### Step 2: Pay Deposit

1. In the booking modal, review the deposit amount
2. Click **"Proceed to Payment"**
3. You'll be redirected to the payment page
4. Enter your payment details (Stripe)
5. Complete the payment

**Note:** The deposit is typically AED 199 (or equivalent in other currencies). This secures your booking and unlocks your calendar for one year.

### Step 3: Select Travel Dates

1. After payment, go to **"My Bookings"** from the dashboard
2. Find your booking and click **"Select Travel Dates"**
3. Choose your preferred start and end dates on the calendar
4. Optionally check **"I'm flexible with these dates (Â±2 days)"**
5. Click **"Confirm Dates"**

**Note:** The calendar is unlocked for one year from your deposit payment date.

### Step 4: Admin Confirmation

- After selecting dates, an admin will review and confirm your booking
- You'll receive notifications about the booking status
- Once confirmed, your travel dates are locked in

### Adding Activities to Your Booking

1. When booking a destination, you can add activities in the booking modal
2. Browse available activities for that destination
3. Click **"View Details"** to see activity information
4. Click **"Add to Booking"** to include activities
5. Activities will be included in your combined payment

## Booking Activities

### Standalone Activity Booking

1. Click **"Activity"** in the navigation bar
2. Browse available activities
3. Click **"Book Now"** on an activity
4. In the calendar modal:
   - Select one or multiple dates
   - Click **"Continue"**
5. Fill in booking details:
   - Number of participants
   - Your name and email
   - Phone number (optional)
   - Special requests (optional)
6. Click **"Proceed to Payment"**
7. Complete payment

### Activity Booking Features

- **Multiple Date Selection:** Select multiple dates for the same activity
- **Price Calculation:** Total price is calculated based on selected dates and participants
- **Availability:** Only available dates are shown on the calendar

## Managing Your Bookings

### Viewing All Bookings

1. Go to **"My Bookings"** from the dashboard or navigation
2. See all your bookings with their current status:
   - **Pending Payment:** Deposit not yet paid
   - **Deposit Paid:** Waiting for date selection
   - **Dates Selected:** Waiting for admin confirmation
   - **Confirmed:** Booking confirmed and ready
   - **Rejected:** Booking was rejected
   - **Cancelled:** Booking was cancelled

### Booking Details

Click on any booking to view:
- Booking reference number
- Destination/Activity information
- Travel dates (if selected)
- Payment details
- Booking status
- Linked activities (if any)

### Actions Available

- **Select Travel Dates:** Choose your travel dates (after deposit payment)
- **View Destination:** Go to destination details page
- **Cancel Booking:** Cancel your booking (if allowed)

## Payment Information

### Supported Payment Methods

- Credit/Debit Cards (via Stripe)
- Secure payment processing

1. **Destination Booking:**
   - Pay deposit (typically AED 199)

- Your payment information is encrypted

## Calendar Features

### Understanding the Calendar

- **Available Dates:** White background - dates you can select
- **Selected Dates:** Orange highlight - your chosen dates
- **Fully Booked:** Red background - no availability
- **Past Dates:** Grayed out - cannot be selected

### Date Selection Tips

- Select a date range for destination bookings
- Select multiple individual dates for activities
- Check availability before selecting
- Use the flexibility option if your dates are flexible

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't log in?**
- Check your email and password
- Use "Forgot Password" if needed
- Ensure your email is verified

**Payment not working?**
- Check your card details
- Ensure sufficient funds
- Try a different payment method
- Contact support if issues persist

**Can't select dates?**
- Ensure deposit is paid
- Check calendar unlock period (1 year from deposit)
- Verify dates are available

**Booking status not updating?**
- Refresh the page
- Check your notifications
- Contact support if needed
